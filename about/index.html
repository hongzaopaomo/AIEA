OUR MISSION

Creating Global
Platforms for
African Artists

The AIEA Art Foundation was established with a clear mission: to support, promote, and preserve African art and culture. We strive to create platforms for African artists to showcase their talent globally while fostering cultural exchange and understanding.

OUR HISTORY

Our Journey

Founded in 2018, the AIEA Art Foundation has grown from a small initiative to a recognized institution in the art world.

2018

Foundation Established

AIEA Art Foundation was established with the vision to promote African art worldwide.

2019

First Exhibition

We organized our first international exhibition featuring 15 African artists.

2020

Digital Expansion

Launched our online platform to reach broader audiences during global challenges.

2022

Global Recognition

Recognized by international art institutions for our contribution to promoting African art.
